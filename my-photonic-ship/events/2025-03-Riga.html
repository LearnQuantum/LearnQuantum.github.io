My Photonic Ship in Riga

A Quantum Workshop for High School Students

About the Workshop

Quantum mechanics is at the heart of cutting-edge technologies, from quantum computers to secure communications. But how can we understand and experience such an abstract and counterintuitive concept?

"My Photonic Ship" is an interactive, hands-on workshop designed for high school students to explore quantum superposition through engaging experiments, programming, and game development.

As part of the 2025 International Year of Quantum Science and Technology, this event offers a unique opportunity to celebrate quantum advancements together while gaining firsthand experience with quantum concepts in action.

What Will You Learn?

  • ✅ Conduct simulated photonic experiments to understand quantum superposition
  • ✅ Implement these experiments using Qiskit, a quantum programming library
  • ✅ Explore the mathematics and philosophy behind quantum mechanics
  • ✅ Develop a classical game and extend it using quantum superposition
  • ✅ Collaborate, discuss, and think creatively about quantum technologies

Who Can Join?

This workshop is open to high school students (grades 9–12) with an interest in science, programming, or technology.

Basic programming experience in Python is required.

But no prior knowledge of quantum computing is necessary!

Why Join?

  • 🔹 Experience quantum superposition firsthand through interactive experiments and hands-on programming.
  • 🔹 Develop real-world skills in game development using Python and Pygame.
  • 🔹 Engage in thought-provoking discussions on the philosophical aspects of quantum mechanics.
  • 🔹 Work on a unique quantum-inspired game project and see quantum concepts in action.
  • 🔹 Collaborate with like-minded peers and develop problem-solving skills.
  • 🔹 Earn a certificate of completion to showcase your knowledge of quantum computing and game development.

Workshop Versions

We are offering two versions of the workshop. The final format will be determined based on participant interest during registration.

📌 7-Hour Version (In-Person Only)

This version provides a deep dive into quantum superposition and game development with Pygame, incorporating quantum superposition concepts into gameplay.

  • Morning:
    • Exploring quantum superposition through photonic experiments using a simulator
    • Developing a classical game with Pygame
  • Afternoon:
    • Quantum Game Development: Hands-on coding and design activity

📌 10-Hour Version

This version includes a self-paced preparation module and an additional online Q&A session before the in-person event.

🖥️ Before the Workshop

  • Self-paced preparation (2-4 hours): "Game Development with Pygame"—familiarize yourself with Pygame basics to maximize your in-person experience.
  • Live Q&A session: Wednesday, March 19, 19:00 – 20:00—get your questions answered before the workshop.

📅 In-Person Workshop

  • Morning:
    • Exploring quantum superposition through photonic experiments using a simulator
    • Implementing these experiments with quantum programming
    • Exploring the mathematical foundations and engaging in philosophical discussions
  • Afternoon:
    • Quantum Game Development: Hands-on coding and design activity
